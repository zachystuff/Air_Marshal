Your mission, as the Air Marshal, is to keep the flight passengers safe. In order to do so you have to determine who
is the criminal. To help you achieve this you will have to go around the plane, to talk to the passengers and crew,
and gather items.

To play the game you will be prompted with what do you what to do. Your options are:

1. Move - this will allow you to move across different sections of the plane. Once you enter move as your option.
          You are provided with the directions you have available to move to. Enter your selection to move to another room.
2. Talk - as you visit each section this will allow you to interact with the passengers and crew. You are provided
          the people you can interact with. Enter the person you want to talk to and see what they have to say.
3. Items - this will allow you to see the items that are in the room and the player will be prompted
           to select which item to collect
4. Inventory - this will display the items the player has collected - "under development"
5. Journal - this will display all the clues the player has collected - "under development"
6. Help - "under development"

To win the game you, as the Air Marshal, have to guess who the criminal is.
